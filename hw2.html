EMINEM


Eminem, by name of Marshall Bruce Mathers III, (born October 17, 1972, St. Joseph, Missouri, U.S.), American rapper,
record producer, and actor who was known as one of the most-controversial and best-selling artists of the early 21st
century. Mathers had a turbulent childhood, marked by poverty and allegations of abuse.


"I am whatever you say I am; if I wasn't, then why would you say I am."
